## What this book is
A **periodized hypertrophy program** built on muscle-growth research. M.A.X. = Mitogen Activated Xtreme. The core idea: cycle through distinct training phases so the body never fully adapts, maximizing the three drivers of muscle growth.

## The three mechanisms of muscle growth (use these to ground gym advice)
1. **Mechanical tension** — lifting challenging loads through a full range of motion. The foundation.
2. **Metabolic stress** — the "burn"/pump from higher-rep, shorter-rest work that floods the muscle.
3. **Muscle damage** — controlled microtrauma (esp. from the lowering/eccentric portion) that signals repair and growth.

A good program hits all three over time rather than only one.

## The periodized structure
The plan cycles **strength → metabolic → muscle (hypertrophy)** phases, with a built-in **deload** week to recover (e.g., "block 2 / week 8 is a deload — train fewer days, lighter, longer rest"). *Application for the app:* progress isn't linear hammering; planned variation + recovery weeks beat doing the same thing forever. The streak should reward smart cycling, not grinding to injury.

## Key training principles
- **Full range of motion** and controlled tempo (especially the eccentric) maximize growth per rep.
- **Effort matters** — train close enough to failure to recruit the muscle, but manage fatigue.
- **Exercise variety** across the program hits muscles from multiple angles.
- **Progressive overload** still underpins everything — add load/reps over the cycle.

## Nutrition
Supports the build: adequate **protein** (spread through the day), enough total calories to grow, and consistency. Pairs with the Beyond Bigger Leaner Stronger nutrition guidance.
