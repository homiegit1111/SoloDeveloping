# MANMADE — Chris Salgardo

**Category:** skincare, grooming (men) · **251 pages · 102 indexed chunks**
President of Kiehl's. The definitive men's grooming reference. Foreword by

## The mantra (memorise this)
> "Cleanse, moisturize, protect. In that order." (p.497)

Three steps, every day, no exceptions. "Even men who are [resistant] … moisturize.
Every time." (p.516)

## Why grooming matters (Foreword, Anthony Mackie p.10)
> "From cleanliness and purity, good things can grow."

> "'Getting ready' is really about 'becoming ready' — focused, prepared — for
> what is to come."

Grooming is the **first intentional act of the day** — you decide how to present
yourself to the world before the world gets a say. A discipline + identity lesson,
not vanity.

## The core routine
- **Cleanse** twice a day with a proper face wash (not bar soap, which strips). (p.497)
- **Moisturise** every time — choose one with gentle, hydrating ingredients. (p.522, p.872)
- **Protect** — finish the morning with a **moisturiser containing SPF** (aim SPF 50+). (p.558, p.878)
- Apply products with the **ring finger** (lightest touch, around the eyes). (p.628)

## Shaving (do it right)
- **Always shave / trim WITH the grain** (direction of hair growth). Make a
  second pass if needed — never hack against the grain first. (p.760, p.733)
- Electric razor: **start dry and clean.** (p.568)
- Prep matters: warm skin, good lather, sharp blade.

## Hands, hair, extras
- **Moisturise hands daily**, especially with manual labour. Salves & balms
  matter. (p.768, p.785)
- Gray hair can look **classic and confident** — own it, keep scalp SPF'd. (p.703, p.702)
- "The Takeaway" (Ch.9) is a one-stop hit list: Hair, Face, Shaving, Facial
  Hair, Eyes, Nails, Hands, Feet, Bodycare/Manscaping, Fragrance. (p.73, p.878+)

## Mindset for Ravi (masculine development crossover)
- Grooming is **self-respect and respect for others** — "caring for myself is how
  I prepare to care for and connect with others." (Mackie, p.10)
- Getting the basics right **early** compounds confidence "sooner in life." (p.11)
- A clean base is where you "grow into anybody you dream of being." (p.11)
