# Glow From Within — Joanna Vargas

**Category:** skincare, nutrition · **151 pages · 124 indexed chunks**
Celebrity facialist Joanna Vargas. Core thesis: *great skin is not genetics or
expensive products — it is a solid, consistent routine plus what you eat.*

## The non-negotiable truth (Intro, p.5)
> "Armed with a strong, reliable, and consistent plan of action, you can enjoy
> gorgeous, glowing skin for the rest of your life."

Skin quality is **not** about skin type or money. It is about a **consistent
routine** + understanding *why* you do each step. (p.5–6)

## The 5 essential steps (everyone, every skin type)
1. **Cleanse** — gentle cleanser that won't strip moisture. Over-cleansing
   damages the barrier. (p.1270, p.1037)
2. **Exfoliate** — supports desquamation (natural shedding). If skin is
   *dehydrated*, exfoliate THEN layer a heavier moisturiser over serum. (p.392, p.897)
3. **Treat** — serum / mask. Ingredients like hyaluronic acid and almond oil
   help boost collagen. (p.567)
4. **Moisturise** — lock in hydration; match weight to your skin's needs.
5. **Protect (SPF)** — sunscreen **daily**, every skin tone, every age. Sun
   exposure ages and harms all skin. (p.479, p.557)

## Nutrition = skincare (Chapter Two)
- **Greens & fruit feed the skin.** Good nutrition is what helps cells get
  stronger and move efficiently — "that's what you can find in greens." (p.20)
- Don't limit to vegetables — many fruits give the skin a boost too.
- Vargas builds whole **juices & masks** around this (see book appendix).

## Mindset for Ravi
- **Know your own skin.** What works for a friend may break *you* out — Vargas
  herself breaks out from marula oil she recommends to clients. (p.71) Experiment
  smartly, observe, adjust.
- Beautiful skin is "doing the same thing, with the same products, morning and
  night, day after day." → This is a **discipline lesson disguised as skincare.**
  Consistency over intensity. Perfect fuel for the Discipline + Skincare habits.

## Daily-planner hooks
- Morning: cleanse → treat → moisturise → SPF.
- Evening: cleanse → exfoliate (2–3×/week) → treat → moisturise.
- Rotating glow tip pool: hydration, greens, SPF discipline, gentle cleansing,
  sleep, not over-exfoliating.
